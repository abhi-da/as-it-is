Tug of War Game Rules

Team Setup

  • The game is played with 8 members from each house in a mixed event.

Winning Conditions

  • The team that crosses the 3-meter marker wins the game.
  • The game is played in a best-of-3 format.

Rope and Pulling Rules

  • The center of the rope must be directly above the mark at the start of the game.
  • The rope must be pulled underarm, and no player's elbow can go below the knee; otherwise, a foul will be called.

Cautions and Fouls

  • If a team member falls, they receive a caution. Each team is allowed two cautions before disqualification.
  • Touching the ground for too long is considered a foul.

Equipment

  • No cleats or gloves are allowed during the event.
  • No anchoring or looping around the end of the rope is permitted.
