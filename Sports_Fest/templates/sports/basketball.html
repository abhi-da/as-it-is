Basketball Rules

This sport will be a mixed team sport. Each team needs to provide 2 female + 2 male players. You can keep as many substitutes as you want, and there will be rolling substitutions.

Definitions

    This sport will be a mixed team sport. Each team needs to provide 2 female + 2 male players. You can keep as many substitutes as you want, and there will be rolling substitutions.

  • Offense team: The team which has the ball is called the offense team.
  • Defense team: The team that tries to stop the offense team from scoring the basket is called the defense team.
  • 3-point line: The outermost curve on the basketball court is called the 3-point line.

Points System

  1. If you score a basket outside the 3-point line, your team will be awarded 3 points (outside the green area and black curve - Figure 1).
  2. If you score from anywhere inside the 3-point line, you will be given 2 points (green area and red area combined - Figure 1).

Game Rules

  • The matches will be 10 minutes long and the game will be half court.
  • There will be a 5-second foul for the offensive team and a 12-second foul for the defensive team. This means:
    • The team with the ball can't stay in the red area (Figure 1) for more than 5 seconds.
    • The defensive team can't stay in the red area (Figure 1) for more than 12 seconds.
  • Since this is a half-court game, in the event of a rebound by the defense team, they must move out to the 3-point line (outside the green area - Figure 2) to make a "clearance" (Figure 2, red arrow) before attempting a basket (Figure 2, blue arrow).
  • Each team can call a maximum of 3 timeouts during the game. Each timeout lasts 45 seconds.

Player Guidelines

  • Players must wear the same jersey colors, and captains must assign numbers to their team players.
  • Captains are advised to register their players using assigned numbers and appoint one player as the game captain.
    • The game captain can decide on rolling substitutions.
  • Players must wear at least jogging shoes. Wearing wristwatches, smartwatches, necklaces, rosaries, wristbands, earrings, etc., is prohibited. Spectacles are allowed.

Gameplay Rules

  • Reaching Fouls: Touching a player holding a ball is a foul, but touching the ball is allowed.
  • Pivot Rule: A player holding the ball with both hands can only move one leg. Moving more than one step is a foul. The player can hold the ball for a maximum of 5 seconds.
  • Double Dribble: Once a player starts dribbling and then holds the ball with both hands, they cannot dribble again but can pass the ball. Double hand dribbling is also a foul.
  • The dribbling player cannot elbow the opponent. Pushing is prohibited, but shoulder pushes while dribbling are allowed.
  • Each player is allowed 5 fouls. Upon the fifth foul, the player is disqualified, and the team captain must send a substitute.

Game Format

  • The game will be conducted in a knockout format as follows:
    • Game 1: Team A vs Team B
    • Game 2: Team C vs Team D
    • 3rd Place Game: Loser of Game 1 vs Loser of Game 2
    • Finals: Winner of Game 1 vs Winner of Game 2
  • The first-round matchups will be decided by a draw of lots.
  • The game will start with a jump ball or toss, depending on the situation.

Additional Notes

The decision of the organizers will be final. For further clarification, feel free to reach out to the organizers.
