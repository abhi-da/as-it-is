Volleyball Game Rules

Team Setup

  • The game will be played with 6 players on each team, with rolling substitutions allowed.
  • Teams must wear matching jerseys with assigned numbers. Players should wear at least jogging shoes.
  • Wearing wristwatches, smartwatches, kadas, necklaces, rosaries, wristbands, or earrings is prohibited. Spectacles are allowed.

Substitutions

  • One player must be assigned as the game captain, who will manage the rolling substitutions.
  • Each team can make unlimited rolling substitutions during the game.

Gameplay

  • Each team is allowed a maximum of 3 touches on the ball before it must be passed to the opponentâ€™s court. Blocks do not count as a touch.
  • The ball cannot be caught, held, or thrown. It may be played off the net during a volley or serve.
  • A ball hitting a boundary line is considered in, and a ball completely outside the court is out.
  • It is illegal to contact the ball with any part of the body except the arm and foot.
  • Both underarm and overarm serves are allowed. If a player touches the boundary line while serving, it will result in a foul and the opponent gains a point.
  • If a player crosses the center line or touches the net during the last move, it will be counted as a foul and the other team is awarded a point.

Match Format

  • Each round consists of 3 sets, and the third place and finals consist of 5 sets.
  • Each set is played to 11 points, with a 2-point difference required to win after the score reaches 10.
  • The team that wins the most sets wins the game.

Knockout Format

  • The knockout format is as follows:
    • Game 1: A vs B
    • Game 2: C vs D
    • 3rd Place Game: Loser Game 1 vs Loser Game 2
    • Finals: Winner Game 1 vs Winner Game 2
  • The first round will be decided by a draw of lots. The first-round starting team will be decided by a coin toss.
  • The organizers' decisions will be final based on the rules specified.
