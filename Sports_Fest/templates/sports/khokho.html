Kho-Kho Game Rules

Game Overview

Kho-Kho is played between two teams, each consisting of 5 players. The game involves two innings, each lasting 9 minutes, which includes both chasing and running.

Team Setup

  • Each team has 5 players.
  • The game consists of two innings.

Chasing Rules

  • The chasing team sits or kneels in the middle of the court in a row, with each player facing opposite sides alternately.
  • The chaser must touch the nearest possible player on the back and say "Kho" to allow them a chance to chase.
  • The team that takes the shortest amount of time to touch the player being chased wins.

Turns

  • The runner or chaser is decided by a toss.
  • The captain of the chasing side may end the turn before the allotted time.
  • If a chaser touches both runners, two new runners from the entry zone enter the field.

Fouls

  • Fouls include early Kho, touching without saying Kho, changing direction while chasing, cutting the cross lane, getting up without receiving a Kho, or late Kho.

Tie Breaker

If there is a tie, an extra turn is performed. The winner of the extra turn is the team that touches one runner from the opposite team in the shortest time.
